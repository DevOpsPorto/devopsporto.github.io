Porto Event Listing

We believe in building bridges between all tech related groups in the region, in order to create more momentum in the tech scene in Porto. The following is a listing of known tech related events in the region, which we hope you will consider participating in:

{% include components/calendarHeader.html year="2018" %}

10 YAKSHA Public Launch Event UP http://web.spi.pt/YAKSHA/ YAKSHA - Cybersecurity Awareness and Knowledge Systemic High-level Application is a project funded by the European Commission and aims at reinforcing EU-ASEAN cooperation & building partnerships in the cybersecurity domain by developing a solution tailored to specific user and national needs, leveraging EU Know-How and local expertise.
20 Talk A Bit - 6th Edition FEUP http://talkabit.org/ Talk A Bit is a non-profit tech conference organized by graduating Software Engineering students from FEUP, the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto. Like in previous occurances, this year's 6th edition of the conference will focus on the social impact of technology.

15 Hello World Conf Univ. Fernando Pessoa https://helloworldconf.pt/ Hello World aims to bring together students from all of the IT related areas. We try to provide you with talks, workshops and networking moments, as we believe these are extremely important in order to keep with the fastly paced technological evolution we live today. We're also trying to get together IT professionals and the companies they work for so you can get in touch with real people and real companies.
