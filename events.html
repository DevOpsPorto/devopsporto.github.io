Porto Event Listing

We believe in building bridges between all tech related groups in the region, in order to create more momentum in the tech scene in Porto. The following is a listing of known tech related events in the region, which we hope you will consider participating in:

10 YAKSHA Public Launch Event UP http://web.spi.pt/YAKSHA/ YAKSHA - Cybersecurity Awareness and Knowledge Systemic High-level Application is a project funded by the European Commission and aims at reinforcing EU-ASEAN cooperation & building partnerships in the cybersecurity domain by developing a solution tailored to specific user and national needs, leveraging EU Know-How and local expertise.
10 Bitcoin, Altcoins & Blockchain Meetup Clube Porto Rugby Meetup link Welcome to the World of Speed Crypto Dating! The audience will be divided into two groups: the most experienced and the less experienced ones. Every element will talk (and drink beer) with an element from the opposite group and will share their experiences, tools, mistakes and other things that may be relevant for the topic. Each 5 minutes you'll trade places to speak with a different person, and your beer glass if you're thirsty. ;)
11 Porto Codes Meetup Porto I/O Downtown Meetup link Porto Codes is a meetup for local and international programming enthusiasts. The talks are recorded and published with the presenters' consent as one of our goals is to provide programming resources to the community
11 Drupal Training Days Meetup Meetup link After several requests for training, we will join the Drupal Association global initiative and host the very first GTD event in Portugal. The training will be free but with limited attendees. We are proposing one main track: "Up and running with Drupal - What is and how to use for end users". We will have two other tracks depending on the attendee's skillsets or interest: "Drupal for developers - From installation to custom development" "Advanced Drupal training - Best practices, performance, and security"
20 Talk A Bit - 6th Edition FEUP http://talkabit.org/ Talk A Bit is a non-profit tech conference organized by graduating Software Engineering students from FEUP, the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto. Like in previous occurances, this year's 6th edition of the conference will focus on the social impact of technology.
31 Agile Connect Porto Meetup Natixis Meetup link This workshop will describe the process used to explore, discover and confirm the value proposition of a new product - CSP Fast Pass (www.cspfastpass.com). At discrete checkpoints, participants will take on the role of Product Owners as they design this product and be asked how they would solve the challenges related to bringing CSP Fast Pass to market. Come to this session to learn how to apply tools and techniques first popularized by Luke Hohmann (Innovation Games®) and Alex Osterwalder (value proposition design and business model canvas) to your own product development challenges. Most of all, bring your product ideas and have fun!
31 Porto Wordpress Meetup Porto I/O Seaside Meetup link 018 será o 'ano Gutenberg' no WordPress. Para os mais distraídos, Gutenberg é o nome do futuro editor de conteúdos do WordPress. Em desenvolvimento há muitos meses, vem revolucionar a forma como criamos conteúdo nos nossos sites. Para muito representa uma forma simples, prática e eficaz de criar conteúdo de forma apelativa, visualmente agradável e rápida. Incluindo conteúdos visuais complexos, permitindo eliminar a existência de um sistema do género 'page builder'. Para muitos outros, em particular programadores de sites e aplicações complexas, representa um desafio. De uma forma ou outra, Gutenberg será um marco determinante no futuro do WordPress. Mais um sinal da sua importância? O lançamento do WordPress 5 está dependente da sua integração no core do sistema.
